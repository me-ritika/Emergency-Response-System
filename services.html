Our Emergency Response systems offer a range of vital services to improve emergency response and save lives which includes
Dynamic Route Planning, Prioritization and Dispatch, Hospital Coordination and Availability , Data Analysis and Improvement.

Basic Life Support (BLS)
These includes providing oxygen therapy,controlling bleeding and wounds, performing CPR and defibrillation,monitoring vital signs like heart rate, blood pressure, and breathing.

Advanced Life Support (ALS)
In addition to BLS, ambulances with ALS capabilities can provide intravenous (IV) fluids and medications, Endotracheal intubation, cardiac monitoring and advanced cardiac interventions, trauma stabilization, stroke interventions.

Hospital Availability
We have services for coordination with hospitals that ensures the patient arrives at the most appropriate facility with immediate treatment capacity.

Ambulance Dispatch
In the event that the ambulance is outside of the five kilometers. After that, we'll head straight to the hospital and send out a backup ambulance.
